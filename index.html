Welcome to St. Raenboe's

Our world is changing and continues to become more competitive. St. Raenboe's is here to provide a solid foundation of technical skills and knowledge to prepare our students to meet those challenges.

In this major transition period in life, students will no doubt have questions about the next steps of securing their education to keep up with the competitive world we live in. Visit one of our community resource fairs, learn more about employment in the world today, and get connected with the larger community through our local library system.


The Best Support a College Can Offer

At St. Raenboe's, we pride ourselves in not only giving our students a top quality education, but also giving them the resources and community every human being needs to survive. This includes food, housing, health insurance, and mental health counseling.
In order to commit to this, we provide these student resources:

  • Financial and Community Support
  • BIPOC Community Center
  • Disability Center
  • LGBTQ+ Center
